# What Analytics207 Is — and What The Model Tries to Show

If you’re used to looking at records, Heal Points, and standings, you’re only seeing part of the picture.

Analytics207 exists to explain what those numbers don’t.

It is a statewide, game-by-game analytical system built specifically for Maine high school basketball. Every varsity game feeds into a single framework designed to show how teams perform relative to their schedule, competition, and timing within the season.

The goal is not to replace standings or official rankings.  
It is to add context to them.

Over time, this site will explore:

- why some records overstate or understate team strength
- how schedule difficulty shapes the standings
- which matchups matter more than they appear
- how the numbers shift as the season unfolds

Basketball is still decided on the floor.

Analytics207 is simply a way to understand what the floor is showing.
